ABOUT

I'm a photographer and designer from Poland. I hold a Master's degree in Graphic Design from the Academy of Fine Arts in Katowice. Throughout my professional career, I have worked as a design advisor for multiple brands such as Lamborghini, Pfizer, Moncler, and Deutsche Telekom. My personal design work has been displayed at multiple exhibitions. Always inspired by documentary films and the work of photojournalists, in recent years I have shifted my focus toward capturing cultures, their traditions, and professions that are slowly disappearing.
