Portfolio / Shipyard

Shipyard

01/04
Year 2024
Category Documentary
Location Poland

A series documenting the daily rhythm of workers at a traditional shipyard. Shot over several visits, the project captures the physicality of manual labour against the industrial backdrop of steel and water — the weight of machinery, the precision of skilled hands, and the quiet moments between shifts.
