ABOUT

Bartek R. is a photographer from Poland whose work focuses on documentary and street photography. After completing a Master's degree in Graphic Design at the Academy of Fine Arts, he gradually shifted his focus toward photography as his primary medium.

His work explores everyday life and local traditions, with a particular interest in observing people at work and the environments shaped by their daily activities. Through travel and close observation, he documents moments that reveal the character of a place and the routines of the people who live there.

Combining a background in visual design with a documentary approach, his photography aims to capture authentic moments while maintaining a strong visual narrative.
